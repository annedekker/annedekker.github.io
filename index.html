Conway's Game of Life

This is an implementation of Conway's Game of Life in C# and WPF. It allows generating random fields, selecting one from a predefined set of templates, or making your own by clicking cells to live in the field.

A wide variety of options are available to the user, including zooming in and out and moving around on the field, setting the game rules, changing the colors, and more.

For more information on the game itself and the program, visit the project page.
