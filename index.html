CHAOS GAME

The chaos game is a method of generating fractals, by deciding on a set of corners, and moving a point a certain fraction of the distance between itself and a random corner every turn.

The most famous fractal is the Sierpinski triangle, but a variety of shapes can be created by changing the settings. (The figure you see on the left is not a fractal, it's just pretty).

GAME OF LIFE

The game of life is a cellular automata, a simulation ran on a grid of cells that turn on or off based on specific rules, that together form a pattern. These games are studied because of their computational power, their useability as physics models, and for the study of patterns. Also, they look cool.
